Space Technologies And Rocketry (Cal STAR):

Air Bears (Fall 2019 Rocket)
This rocket pioneered the dual-deploy structure applied in the full scale Bear Force One Rocket we plan to fly in the Spaceport America Cup (IREC). Airbears was launched twice, once at baseline with a L1250 motor, and once with a 9 lb payload at transonic range on an L1150 motor to simulate IREC conditions.


In Progress! Bear Force One (Spring 2020 Rocket)
This rocket is designed to spec for the 10,000 ft apogee COTS division of the Intercollegiate Rocketry Engineering Competition (IREC). It has a fiberglass airframe, dual deployment recovery, and carries an 8.8 lb payload.
